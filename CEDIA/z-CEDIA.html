Seamless Best Integrated Home, Winner
Situated in Canada and crafted in collaboration with Iredale Architecture and Rockridge Fine Homes, Seamless by La Scala Integrated Media transforms a once sleepy neighborhood into a finely tuned ensemble of domestic bliss and technological sophistication.

The homeowner, serving as the interior designer, infused the residence with a personal touch that speaks volumes of the intricacies involved in creating a home where every element is thoughtfully interconnected.

Rejecting numerous overtures from developers, the client waited for the perfect opportunity to combine two lots into a sprawling one-acre estate, thus crafting a retirement sanctuary complete with a social kitchen, media room, and a sports court set 230 feet (70 meters) away. La Scala's execution pivots on the principle that optimal functionality arises not from excess but from precision. By reducing the planned 20 audio zones down to eight, reflecting actual usage, the project highlights the virtue of restraint and the importance of tailoring technology to fit daily life.
