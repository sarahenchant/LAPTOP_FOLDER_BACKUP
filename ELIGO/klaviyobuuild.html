The Race to Madrid returns in 2026
The Order of Merit returns for its second year, culminating in a final to be held in Madrid from 5th–7th October.
 
The competition will be decided by each player’s best 3 scores from 6 stableford rounds and will run from the first week of the Winter Sanctuary through to 31st August.
 
All events will open for entry on the App from Monday 12th January 12.00.
