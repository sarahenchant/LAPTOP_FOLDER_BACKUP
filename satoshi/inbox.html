The official Trump memecoin was released on the Solana network. You can activate Solana network in the Settings > Coins.



Then, use the Solana receiving address to send the coins into your Trezor. They will appear in your Solana account.

If you would like to send your Trump coins out of your Trezor, you will need to have Solana (the native coin) in the account as well to be able to pay for the fees. You can either send the Solana in your Trezor via a transaction or you can buy Solana directly in your Trezor Suite using the Buy feature. Let me know if you would have further questions.
